MBA 534 • Digital Marketing & Analytics

Welcome to MBA 534

Taught by Professor Jim O'Farrell — explore digital marketing foundations, strategy, analytics, and the role of AI in shaping modern marketing management.

Instructor: Prof. Jim O'Farrell, Ph.D.
Email: ofarrelljames@cityu.edu
Schedule: Fall 2025 — Mondays 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM PDT
Credits: 3 | Grading: Decimal
Course Site: Brightspace

Learning Outcomes

  • Apply digital marketing techniques to target market development and brand management, incorporating AI tools.
  • Analyze value concepts and apply them to marketing of products and services, leveraging AI-driven insights.
  • Synthesize digital marketing strategy, research, and planning concepts using AI for data-driven decision-making.
  • Evaluate marketing analytics platforms, including AI applications for Big Data, CRM, and program management.

Assessment & Grading

  • Target Market & Brand Management Report — 15%
  • “My Dream Product” Video Project — 15%
  • Digital Marketing Plan (DMP) Presentation — 20%
  • Managing the Digital Marketing Program Team Presentation — 30%
  • Instructor Determined Assignments & Activities — 20%
