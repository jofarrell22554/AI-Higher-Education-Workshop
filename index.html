Course Overview

This course equips you with a cross‑functional understanding of how organizations create value. You will analyze cases, practice data‑driven decision making, and collaborate on a capstone project that integrates strategy, marketing, operations, and finance.

  • Blend qualitative frameworks with quantitative analysis.
  • Translate insights into executive‑level recommendations.
  • Build leadership skills through structured team collaboration.
Tip: Skim the case before class; re‑read with the week’s frameworks to prepare for discussion.

Learning Outcomes

  1. Diagnose business problems using strategy, marketing, ops, and finance lenses.
  2. Apply basic analytics (descriptive, diagnostic) to support decisions.
  3. Communicate recommendations clearly to executive stakeholders.
  4. Collaborate effectively in diverse teams using project best practices.
  5. Reflect on ethical, global, and sustainability considerations in decisions.

Capstone Project

Teams consult for a real or simulated client. Deliverables: a 6–8 page executive memo, an appendix with analysis, and a 10‑minute briefing. Milestones: topic proposal (Wk 3), check‑in (Wk 6), draft (Wk 8), final (Wk 10).

  • Scope: problem framing, alternatives, recommendation, implementation plan
  • Analysis: market sizing, process/ops, unit economics, risks & mitigations
  • Deliver: concise visuals; plain‑English narrative; action plan

FAQ

Do I need prior business experience?

No. We’ll establish shared vocabulary and build from first principles.

What’s the workload like?

Expect 5–7 hours per week outside class for reading, prep, and team work.

How do teams get formed?

We’ll set teams in Week 2 to balance experience, skills, and interests.
